# 🛡️ Stay Safe Online – Checklist for College Students (India)

- [ ] Use strong, unique passwords for each account (avoid using your name, DOB, or “123456” — hackers love those).
- [ ] Enable **2-factor authentication (2FA)** on email, banking, and social media accounts.
- [ ] Never share OTPs, passwords, or PINs — not even with someone claiming to be from your bank.
- [ ] Avoid using public Wi-Fi for banking or sensitive logins (your data can be intercepted).
- [ ] Check URLs carefully before logging in (look for “https://” and avoid fake lookalike sites).
- [ ] Do not click on suspicious links from unknown emails, WhatsApp, or SMS.
- [ ] Keep your apps, phone, and antivirus updated to avoid security vulnerabilities.
- [ ] Limit personal information shared on social media (location, phone number, college ID, etc.).

## 💰 UPI & Financial Safety Tips
- [ ] Never approve **“Collect Requests”** on UPI unless you are expecting money (approving = sending money).
- [ ] Do not scan random QR codes to receive money — scanning always means you are paying.
- [ ] Verify payment requests even if they come from known contacts (accounts can be hacked).
- [ ] Use official apps only (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm) — avoid APK downloads.

## 🚨 In Case of Cyber Fraud
- Report immediately at: https://cybercrime.gov.in
- Call the Cyber Crime Helpline: 1930
